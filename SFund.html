Pitch

For students who believe that higher education is becoming less affordable, Student Fund is a one stop shop aggregating all your funding needs. We provide an alternative low interest loan with repayment based on a fixed percentage of your income allowing you to repay JUST principle NOT accrued interest.

Features

  • Provide Angel Investor and University Funding on Income Share Based Agreement.
  • Predict future pay package depending on multiple factors.
  • Provide best possible loan repayment solution.
  • Provide end to end expense calculator that covers pre and post college expense.
